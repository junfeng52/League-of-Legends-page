ABILITIES

Pasive

Lightslinger

Whenever Lucian uses an ability, his next attack becomes a double-shot. When Lucian is healed or shielded by an ally, or when a nearby enemy Champion is immobilized, his next 2 basic attacks will deal bonus magic damage.

Ability Q

Piercing Light

Lucian shoots a bolt of piercing light through a target.

Ability W

Ardent Blaze

Lucian shoots a missile that explodes in a star shape, marking and briefly revealing enemies. Lucian gains Move Speed for attacking marked enemies.

Ability E

Relentless Pursuit

Lucian quickly dashes a short distance. Lightslinger attacks reduce Relentless Pursuit's cooldown.

Ability R

The Culling

Lucian unleashes a torrent of shots from his weapons.
