THE STONEWEAVER
TALIYAH

ABILITIES

Pasive

Rock Surfing

Taliyah gains Move Speed near walls.

Ability Q

Threaded Volley

Taliyah throws a volley of rocks in a target direction, moving freely as she casts. This creates Worked Ground below her. If Taliyah casts Threaded Volley on Worked Ground, she consumes the ground to throw a stronger boulder that slows enemies.

Ability W

Seismic Shove

Taliyah causes an area of ground to erupt and throws enemies within it in a direction of her choosing.

Ability E

Unraveled Earth

Taliyah creates a slowing minefield. If enemies dash through it or are knocked through it, the mines explode and stun them.

Ability R

Weaver's Wall

Taliyah creates a very long wall and then surfs it.
