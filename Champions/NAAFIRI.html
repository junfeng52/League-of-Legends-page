THE HOUND OF A HUNDRED BITES
NAAFIRI

ABILITIES

Pasive

We Are More

Naafiri spawns packmates that attack the targets of her attacks and abilities.

Ability Q

Darkin Daggers

Naafiri hurls up to two daggers, each inflicting bleed, or each inflicting bonus damage if the target is already bleeding.

Packmates leap to attack champions hit by this skill.

Ability W

Hounds' Pursuit

Naafiri and her packmates dash at an enemy, colliding with the first champion hit and dealing damage.

Ability E

Eviscerate

Naafiri dashes and damages enemies in an area around her, recalling her packmates and fully healing them.

Ability R

The Call of the Pack

Naafiri empowers her pack and spawns additional packmates, instantly receiving a burst of speed and vision and gaining a shield the first time she hits another champion.

After killing another champion for the first time, all effects are refreshed.
